Hyundai is a South Korean multinational automotive manufacturer, headquartered in Seoul, founded in 1967. Part of the Hyundai Motor Group, the company has a major presence globally, known for vehicles under its own brand, the luxury brand Genesis, and significant ownership of Kia Corporation. Hyundai is a major global automaker recognized for its technology-driven approach to future mobility, including hydrogen-powered vehicles and autonomous driving.
