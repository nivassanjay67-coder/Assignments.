Steps to make Chicken Rice

    Ingredients

  1. 2 cups rice (jasmine or long-grain)

  2. 500 g (about 1 lb) chicken thighs or breasts, cut into chunks

  3. 1 medium onion, diced

  4. 3 cloves garlic, minced

  5. 1 tbsp soy sauce

  6. 1 tbsp oyster sauce (optional)

  7. 1 tsp sesame oil (optional)

  8. 3 cups chicken broth (or water + bouillon cube)

  9. 1 tbsp vegetable oil

  10. Salt and pepper to taste

  11. Green onions or cilantro for garnish

    Instructions

  1. Prepare the rice:

    2. Rinse rice under cold water until the water runs clear, then drain.

  2. Sauté the aromatics:

    3. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat.

    Add onion and garlic, and cook until fragrant and lightly golden.

  3. Add the chicken:

    4. Stir in chicken pieces, season with salt and pepper, and cook until the surface turns white (doesn’t need to be fully cooked yet).

  4. Add sauces:

    5. Stir in soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil if using.

  5. Add rice and liquid:

    6. Pour in the rice and mix well to coat it in the flavors.

    Add chicken broth and stir once.

  6. Cook:

    7. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low. Cover and simmer for 15–20 minutes, or until rice is cooked and liquid is absorbed.

  7. Rest and serve:

    8. Turn off the heat and let it rest (covered) for 5 minutes. Fluff the rice, garnish with green onions or cilantro, and serve warm.
